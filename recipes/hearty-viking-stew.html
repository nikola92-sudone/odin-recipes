Hearty Viking Stew

Description

This hearty Viking stew is rich, rustic, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef chunks
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups broth
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Heat the pot and brown the beef.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until softened.
  3. Pour in broth and seasonings.
  4. Simmer for 1 hour until tender.
